Dragon

Dragons are mythological creatures, not a real animal.

  • Scientific Name: Draconus Rex
  • Max Height: 9 ft.
  • Overall Lenght: 33 ft.
  • Races: There are 50 races of dragons

A dragon is a large, serpent-like legendary creature that appears in the folklore of many cultures around the world. ... The popular western image of a dragon as winged, four-legged, and capable of breathing fire is an invention of the High Middle Ages based on a conflation of earlier dragons from different traditions.
